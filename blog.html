BEST MOBILE PHONE IN WORLD

Smartphones have become so entangled with our daily lives that the difference between a good smartphone that just works and a bad smartphone with lots of bugs, can make or break the day for you. And while most smartphones have something good to offer, the best phones offer the right balance between the hardware and software, offering lots of computing power and effortless usability. With the best mobiles available in India, you can do just about anything - From playing a graphics-heavy game to watching a movie in HDR quality to editing a spreadsheet or making a video, the best smartphones are all-rounders in every sense. In 2020, the best mobile phones are powered by Apple's iOS and Google's Android operating system, with most flagships running on the latest Android 10. These smartphones take sharp, detailed photos, heavy multi-tasking and a host of features that define cutting edge in mobile technology. Check out our list of the top 10 mobiles to buy in India in 2020.

IPHONE 11PRO

The iPhone 11 Pro is one of the best mobile phones in India. Alongside that, it is one of the most expensive smartphones in India that you can buy right now. It is powered by Apple's latest A13 Bionic chip. You get an excellent 5.8-inch HDR10 display that tops at 800 nits. On the back lies a 12MP triple camera setup, that comes equipped with a telephoto lens as well as an ultra-wide-angle sensor. It uses data from all the three lenses while shooting a video. iPhone 11 Pro is one of the best when it comes to video shooting capabilities. It is certainly one of the best mobile phones in India right now.

SAMSUNG GALAXY S20 ULTRA

The Galaxy S20+ is one of the most beefed-up premium Android phone you can buy, and there's a lot of features that takes months to explore. Inside is the new Exynos 990 coupled with up to 16GB RAM and 512GB storage. The S20 Ultra also rocks a 108MP primary camera coupled with a 48MP periscope telephoto camera along with an ultrawide and ToF 3D sensor. On the front, you get what is perhaps the most well-tuned display measuring 6.9-inches with QHD+ resolution, 120Hz refresh rate and HDR10+ certification.
